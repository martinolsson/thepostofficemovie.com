We made a puppet movie!

In 1912 Nobel Prize author Rabindranath Tagore wrote "The Post Office". In 2015 a group of pupeteers, actors and artists led by Silvia Bergman envisioned the play as a puppet movie with Swedish actors.

The 40 minute movie is free to watch, and we even made English subtitles. If you like it please donate so we can make an English dialogue version.

Enjoy the movie!

Support the project

We made this movie out of love. If you love it too, please spread this link: www.thepostofficemovie.com. The movie is free to watch, but please consider donating €1, €5, €15 or €100 using the button on the right.
